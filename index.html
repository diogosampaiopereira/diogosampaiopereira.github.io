About Me

Hi! My name is Diogo Pereira and I recently started a PhD in Materials Engineering at Instituto Superior Técnico . I am integrated in the Materials Processing and Characterisation group at the Instituto de Plasma e Fusão Nuclear (IPFN) , under the supervision of Doctor Rodrigo Mateus and Doctor Norberto Catarino .

In collaboration with the National Institute for Laser, Plasma and Radiation Physics (INFLPR) in Romania, my research focuses on investigating the oxidation behaviour of boron coatings for nuclear fusion applications.
